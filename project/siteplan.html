Togolese Art and Culture

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: Togolese Art and Culture

I chose this name to highlight the richness of Togo’s cultural heritage and artistic traditions.

Site Purpose

The site introduces Togolese arts and culture, showcases a gallery of paintings and sculptures, and provides information about traditions and artists. It also includes a contact form and references page.

Scenarios

  • Where can I see examples of Togolese paintings and sculptures?
  • How can I contact the artists or learn more about Togolese traditions?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Dark Green (#006400) — used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: Gold (#FFD700) — used for highlights, buttons, and footer.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (modern, clean for titles)

Body Font: Roboto (easy to read for paragraphs)

Wireframes

Mobile View

The mobile layout will have a header with the site title "Togolese Art and Culture" and a menu icon. The main content will be stacked vertically, showing images of Togolese paintings and sculptures with short descriptions. The footer will be at the bottom of the page.

Desktop View

The desktop layout will include a header with a horizontal navigation menu. The main content will be displayed in two or three columns to showcase artwork images alongside cultural information. The footer will span across the bottom of the page.
